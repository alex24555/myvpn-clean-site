Privacy Policy for MyVPN Clean

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

MyVPN Clean is a VPN client application designed to help users manage VPN profiles and establish secure VPN connections.

1. Data Collection

MyVPN Clean does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

The app does not use advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party advertising services.

2. VPN Profiles and Configuration Data

VPN profiles, subscription links, access tokens, and imported configuration data are stored locally on the user's device.

This data is used only to configure and start the VPN connection selected by the user.

MyVPN Clean does not upload VPN profiles or configuration data to any servers.

3. VPN Traffic

When the VPN connection is active, network traffic is handled according to the VPN profile selected by the user.

MyVPN Clean does not inspect, store, or sell browsing history, DNS history, or traffic content.

4. App Automation

The app may provide automation actions such as connecting or disconnecting VPN. These actions run locally on the device.

5. Third-Party Services

MyVPN Clean does not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

6. Data Retention

All data remains on the user's device until deleted by the user.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page.
